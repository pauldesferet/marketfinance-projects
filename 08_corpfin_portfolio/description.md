CorpFin_ESCPS1.xlsm — CAC 40 Portfolio Management

Excel workbook built as part of a Corporate Finance project at ESCP Business School. It covers the full quantitative analysis of a 12-stock CAC 40 portfolio over 191 monthly observations (August 2010 – June 2026), with data automatically pulled from Yahoo Finance via a Python pipeline (import_yahoo.py).

The workbook includes adjusted closing prices, monthly returns, a full correlation and covariance matrix, individual stock sheets with CAPM regressions (β, α, R²), and Markowitz portfolio optimisation using Excel Solver — covering the minimum variance portfolio, tangency portfolio, and efficient frontier both with and without short sales constraints. A separate sheet analyses the CAC 40 benchmark alongside a French interbank rate series used as the risk-free proxy.
